Our Vision and Goals

Vision
1. Empower students with knowledge and skills in free and open-source.

2. Encourage innovation, creativity, and collaboration.

3. Establish a culture of using open-source alternatives in the college.

4. Equip students with industry-relevant open-source tools.

5. Promote sustainable technology solutions within the college community.
Goals
* FOSS Awareness: Educate students and faculty on the advantages of open-source software.

* Skill Development: Provide training in open-source tools widely used in the industry.

* Contribution to FOSS Projects: Encourage students to participate in global FOSS projects.

* Community Building: Create a collaborative network of students, faculty, and professionals around FOSS.

* Foster Innovation: Inspire students to develop their own open-source solutions to real-world issues.

What We Offer

For Students
1. Hands-on learning through real-world project contributions.

2. Enhanced career opportunities and resume-building through FOSS involvement.

3. Skills in coding, version control, and software testing.

4. Global exposure through collaborations in international FOSS communities.
For Faculty
* Opportunities to integrate FOSS tools into the curriculum.

* A platform for research and academic publications.

* Collaborative projects that improve student mentorship and engagement.
For the Institution
* Recognition in the tech and open-source communities.

* An innovation hub encouraging student creativity.

* Cost efficiency by reducing dependency on proprietary software.
